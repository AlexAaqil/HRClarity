HRClarity

Human Resource Management made easy

Leaves

Making leave requests has never been easier. With just a few details to fill in, your request is sent and ready to be reviewed by the HR.

Announcements

HR managers can easily make announcements viewable by all employees in the organization.

Employees

Verifying, viewing and updating employees data has never been easier. Experience the power of easily searching through all the employees in the organization.

The project was inspired by the struggle observed when working with a small organization that needed a HR software but the only available solutions were premium versions of very expensive HR software.

Despite the high pricing, the software didn't really offer an efficient way for employees to get registered into the system. Thus the creation of HRClarity to ease the process of employees being registered into the system and also making it easy to perform HR functions such as approving or rejecting leave requests.
